About the Project Shop!

Robots are just everywhere!

Robots are a diverse bunch. Some walk around on their two, four, six, or more legs, while others can take to the skies. Some robots help physicians to do surgery inside your body; others toil away in dirty factories. There are robots the size of a coin and robots bigger than a car. Some robots can make pancakes. Others can land on Mars. This diversity—in size, design, capabilities—means it’s not easy to come up with a definition of what a robot is.

Our Mission

As a Robotics Enthusiast, I believe that this is the right platform for students of any age to learn about Robotics! Making this platform will be the best thing in your life, for it will help you learn about so many things there is to know about Robotics. Our Blogs will act as daily digestables for your mind.
